Hello there! I am Cleo and welcome to my site. :)

I am a developer (mostly for the web, but occasionally for other stuff too.) I do Full Stack Web Development using JavaScript and MERN (MongoDB, Express, ReactJS, NodeJS) is my main web stack. I graduated with a Computer Science degree from the University of the Philippines.
